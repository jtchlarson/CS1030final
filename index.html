About

RhythmHouse is an adaptive chore scheduling app that helps households stay organized and stress-free by fitting chores naturally into each family member’s daily routine. Using smart scheduling, it automatically assigns and adjusts tasks based on availability, preferences, and changing schedules — so no one has to waste time figuring out what needs to be done. With a friendly, easy-to-use dashboard, gentle reminders, and progress tracking, RhythmHouse turns everyday chores into a smooth, cooperative process that works for kids and adults alike.
